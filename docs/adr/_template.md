# NNNN — Title

One-sentence tagline summarising the decision. Codesight's knowledge index grabs this line as the entry description, so make it informative.

## Problem

Two or three sentences. What was happening (concrete, often a real example) and what about it forced a decision. If there's no genuine problem, this isn't an ADR.

## Decision

The rule, stated as an imperative. One paragraph.

## Why

Why this decision over the alternatives — what it preserves, what it makes structurally impossible, what reading or reviewing experience it serves. The single most important section. If you can't articulate the why crisply, the decision isn't ready.

## Alternatives considered

Only list alternatives a senior developer would defend on a different project. No strawmen, no "we could do nothing."

- **Real alternative A:** what it is, who would pick it, why it's wrong here.
- **Real alternative B:** same.

## Consequences

What you get, what it costs. Brief.
